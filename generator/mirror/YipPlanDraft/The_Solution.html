The Solution

Imagine a platform where you can discover and monitor events spanning the entire spectrum of human interest.

Everything from the local and niche-specific to the global and far-reaching events. Contrast:

  • Northern Line Closure, 14:00 - 19:00. Bus replacement service running.

To this:

  • Snowden seeks asylum in Russia

Seemingly nothing ties these two events together. But, in fact, they are both the same type of thing -- things happening right now that humans want to know about.

A platform that allows the reporting and discovery of all different types of events would be hugely beneficial to people in all walks of life.

Yip is...

Yip is a platform allowing anyone to add, discover and popularize the events that they are most interested in or affected by.

Yip aggregates event data both automatically and via manual input from users. Read more about Sourcing.

Yip is interested in amassing events of all types. It is not simply an entertainment/leisure website. Yip wants to be more than that, facilitating the discovery of all events that affect humans, both positive and negative.
